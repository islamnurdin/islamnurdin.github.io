What is Artifex?

Artifex is an open-source AI-powered engineering assistant designed to revolutionize the manufacturing process. It streamlines Finite Element Analysis (FEA), Computer-Aided Design (CAD), and prototyping by automating design iterations, optimizing workflows, and reducing manual overhead.
